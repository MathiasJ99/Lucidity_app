Trademark registration simplified

Every business should have full ownership of their brand; whether it’s a name, tagline, stylised design, or a combination of all of these. We understand that registering a trademark can feel overwhelming. Hiring a lawyer is costly, and navigating the process on your own can be time-consuming and risky. That’s why we’re dedicated to providing clear step-by-step guidance to help simplify the process for you to successfully register your UK trademark. No lawyers, no hourly rates and no hidden costs. Just clear advice, guidance and support.

Transparent pricing

Our fixed fees provide transparency and predictability in pricing. Unlike hourly billing, which can lead to unexpected costs, fixed fee pricing ensures that you know the total cost upfront, helping you to budget more effectively. As you move through the step-by-step trade mark application process, the total cost will be visible so that you are able to see clearly how changes to your trade mark application may affect the total cost.

Free and ongoing support

If the trademark office raises any objections, our experienced team will review the issues and work to resolve them for you. We'll keep you in the loop throughout the entire process.
